### 3.2 Internal Linking Architecture

**Current Problem:** Pages exist but aren't properly linked to form topic clusters

**Solution - Link Patterns:**

1. **Pillar to Cluster:** Each pillar page has 15+ outbound links to supporting content
2. **Cluster to Pillar:** Each supporting page links back to pillar in first paragraph
3. **Cluster to Cluster:** Related pages link to 2-3 complementary pages
4. **Breadcrumb Navigation:** All pages show logical hierarchy
5. **Related Articles Section:** 3-5 related articles at bottom of each page

**Example - Electric Cabs Pillar Page Links:**
```
Introduction → Links to Hinjewadi page
Why Electric → Links to Vehicle pages + Cost comparison
How to Book → Links to Booking guide + FAQ
Cost Analysis → Links to Comparison pages
Benefits → Links to Environmental impact blog
Vehicle Options → Links to Kia and BYD pages
Service Areas → Links to local area pages
FAQ → Internal cross-references
CTA → Links to booking page
```
