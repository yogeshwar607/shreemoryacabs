## Directory Submission Tips

1. **Consistency:** Use exact same business name, phone, address across ALL directories
2. **Categories:** Choose most specific category available (avoid generic "Services")
3. **Photos:** Upload minimum 5 photos for each listing (vehicles, driver, route, booking screen)
4. **Descriptions:** Customize for each platform but maintain core message
5. **Keywords:** Include "NMIA", "Navi Mumbai Airport", "Pune", "Electric Cab" in all descriptions
6. **Response Time:** Reply to inquiries within 2 hours (shows active business)
7. **Reviews:** Request 5-star reviews from first 10-20 customers (builds trust)
8. **Updates:** Update listings monthly with new services/offers
9. **Verification:** Complete all verification steps (phone, email, business documents)
10. **Premium Listings:** Consider paid listings on JustDial, Sulekha for better visibility
