## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Profile suspended
**Solution:** Request reinstatement via GBP dashboard. Ensure all info is accurate and complies with Google policies.

### Issue: Reviews not showing up
**Solution:** Reviews can take 24-48 hours to appear. If delayed beyond that, contact Google Business support.

### Issue: Photos being rejected
**Solution:** Ensure photos are:
- High resolution (min 720x720px)
- Not watermarked with competitor logos
- Not blurry or low quality
- Relevant to your business
- Not violating Google's photo policies

### Issue: Competitor using similar name
**Solution:** If it violates trademark, report via GBP. Otherwise, differentiate through better content and reviews.

### Issue: Negative review from fake account
**Solution:** Flag as inappropriate. Provide evidence if available. Respond professionally regardless.
