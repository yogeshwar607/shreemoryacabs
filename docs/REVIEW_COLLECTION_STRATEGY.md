## When to Request Reviews

### ‚úÖ BEST Times to Ask (High Success Rate)

1. **Immediately After Excellent Service**
   - Passenger compliments during ride
   - Smooth, on-time pickup
   - Professional driver behavior
   - Comfortable journey with no issues
   - **Success Rate:** 60-70%

2. **After First-Time Customer's Trip**
   - New customers are excited about the experience
   - Especially if they switched from competitor (GrEL, Ola, Uber)
   - **Success Rate:** 50-60%

3. **After Corporate/Repeat Customer Completes Multiple Trips**
   - Ask after 5th trip: "You've traveled with us 5 times now..."
   - **Success Rate:** 40-50%

4. **After Long-Distance/Special Trips**
   - Outstation trips (Shirdi, Lonavala, Mahabaleshwar)
   - Wedding/event transportation
   - Airport transfers with flight tracking
   - **Success Rate:** 55-65%

### ‚ùå AVOID Asking (Low Success Rate / Damage Risk)

1. **After Service Issues**
   - Delayed pickup
   - Route confusion
   - Vehicle issues
   - Driver behavior complaints
   - **Result:** Risk of negative review

2. **During Busy Hours**
   - Passenger in rush for flight/meeting
   - Multiple bookings waiting
   - **Result:** Ignored request

3. **Too Soon After Trip**
   - Wait at least 5-10 minutes after drop-off
   - Let passenger settle in before asking
   - **Result:** Lower response rate

4. **After Complaint or Issue Resolution**
   - Even if resolved well, wait 1 week
   - Then request review mentioning resolution
   - **Result:** Potentially mixed review

## How to Request Reviews

### Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1: Identify Review-Worthy Trip
During/after trip, driver notes:
- Passenger satisfaction level: High/Medium/Low
- Any compliments given
- Conversation quality
- First-time or repeat customer

#### Step 2: Driver Reports to Operations
Driver messages operations team:
```
Trip completed: Pune Hinjewadi to Mumbai Airport
Passenger: Mr. Sharma (+91 98765 43210)
Satisfaction: HIGH - Passenger said "Best cab experience"
First-time customer: Yes (switched from Ola)
Review request: RECOMMENDED
```

#### Step 3: Operations Sends Review Request
Within 5-10 minutes of trip completion, operations team sends WhatsApp message (see templates below)

#### Step 4: Follow-up (if no response in 24 hours)
Send gentle reminder (see templates below)

#### Step 5: Thank Customer After Review
Once review is posted, send personalized thank you message

## Review Response Guidelines

### Why Respond to Reviews?

- Shows you care about customer feedback
- Improves business credibility
- Boosts SEO (responses are indexed by Google)
- Turns negative experiences into positive outcomes
- Encourages more people to leave reviews

### Response Framework

#### 5-Star Reviews (Respond within 24 hours)

**Template:**
```
Thank you so much, [Customer Name]! üåü

We're thrilled you had a wonderful experience with GreenEVCabs. [Personalize: mention specific detail from their review, e.g., "We're glad you appreciated the spacious SUV and smooth ride!"]

Looking forward to serving you again on your next trip!

- GreenEVCabs Team
```

**Personalization Tips:**
- Quote specific phrase from review
- Mention driver by name if they praised driver
- Reference the route they traveled
- If they compared to competitor, thank them for choosing you

---

#### 4-Star Reviews (Respond within 12 hours)

**Template:**
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Customer Name]!

We're glad you had a good experience with GreenEVCabs. We'd love to know what we can do to make your next ride a 5-star experience! Please feel free to share any suggestions at +91 74982 64215.

Looking forward to serving you again soon!

- GreenEVCabs Team
```

**Action:** Proactively call customer within 24 hours to understand improvement areas

---

#### 3-Star Reviews (Respond within 6 hours)

**Template:**
```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for taking the time to share your experience. We appreciate your feedback, and we'd like to better understand how we can improve.

Could you please reach out to us at +91 74982 64215 so we can discuss your experience and make things right?

We're committed to providing the best service possible.

- GreenEVCabs Management
```

**Action:** Call customer immediately, offer partial refund or free next trip if issue is valid

---

#### 1-2 Star Reviews (Respond within 2-4 hours)

**Template:**
```
Dear [Customer Name],

We sincerely apologize that we did not meet your expectations. This is absolutely not the standard of service we strive for at GreenEVCabs.

We take your concerns very seriously and would like to make this right. Please contact us directly at +91 74982 64215 so we can discuss what went wrong and how we can resolve it.

Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We are committed to improving.

- [Your Name], GreenEVCabs Management
```

**Critical Actions:**
1. Call customer within 2 hours (personal apology from manager)
2. Understand the issue in detail
3. Offer appropriate compensation (full refund, free trip, etc.)
4. Document what went wrong internally
5. Implement corrective measures
6. Follow up with customer after 1 week to ensure satisfaction
7. If issue is resolved and customer is satisfied, politely ask if they'd consider updating review (DO NOT pressure)

**Never:**
- Argue with customer in public response
- Blame the customer
- Make excuses
- Ignore the review
- Delete or report legitimate negative reviews

## Legal & Ethical Guidelines

### Google's Review Policies (MUST Follow)

**‚úÖ ALLOWED:**
- Asking customers to leave honest reviews
- Responding to all reviews
- Incentivizing review (not specifically positive reviews)
- Following up once if no response

**‚ùå PROHIBITED:**
- Paying for positive reviews
- Offering incentives specifically for 5-star reviews
- Posting fake reviews (from employees, friends, fake accounts)
- Asking customers to remove negative reviews
- Gate-keeping (selectively asking only happy customers)
- Bulk uploading reviews from email lists

**Consequences of Violation:**
- Reviews removed by Google
- Business profile suspended
- Loss of ranking
- Permanent ban

### GreenEVCabs Review Ethics Policy

**Our Commitment:**
1. We will only request HONEST reviews from real customers
2. We will incentivize review-leaving, not positive reviews specifically
3. We will respond professionally to ALL reviews, positive and negative
4. We will never post fake reviews or ask others to do so
5. We will never pressure customers for positive reviews
6. We will use feedback to improve service, not manipulate ratings

**Employee Guidelines:**
- Drivers can verbally mention reviews but not force
- Operations can send max 2 messages (1 request + 1 reminder)
- Management will personally handle all 1-3 star reviews
- No employee should leave a review for their own company
