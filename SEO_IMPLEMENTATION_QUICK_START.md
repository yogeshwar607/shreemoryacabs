## STEP-BY-STEP: HOW TO CREATE ONE PIECE OF CONTENT

### Example: "Electric Cabs Pune Complete Guide" (Pillar Page)

#### Step 1: Research (30 mins)
- [ ] Search "electric cab pune" on Google
- [ ] Note top 10 results and their angle
- [ ] Search "ev taxi pune" and similar variations
- [ ] Read 2-3 competitor pages
- [ ] Note common questions users ask
- [ ] Gather any statistics/data available

#### Step 2: Outline (30 mins)
```
# Electric Cabs Pune Complete Guide (4,000 words)

1. Introduction (300 words)
   - Hook with stat: "X% of Pune residents prefer eco-friendly transport"
   - Problem: Pollution, high costs of regular cabs
   - Solution: Electric cabs
   - What you'll learn: 8 sections below

2. What Are Electric Cabs? (400 words)
   - Definition
   - How they work
   - Benefits of battery technology
   - Link to vehicle pages

3. Why Choose Electric Cabs in Pune (500 words)
   - Benefit 1: Cost savings (provide numbers)
   - Benefit 2: Environmental impact
   - Benefit 3: Comfort and reliability
   - Benefit 4: Professional drivers
   - Real testimonials

4. How Much Do Electric Cabs Cost? (400 words)
   - Pricing table
   - Comparison to regular cabs
   - Discounts available
   - Internal link to pricing page

5. Areas We Serve (300 words)
   - List all areas
   - Link to each local area page
   - Google Map embed
   - Coverage details

6. Our Fleet (400 words)
   - Kia Carens EV details
   - BYD eMax 7 details
   - Comparison table
   - Link to vehicle pages

7. How to Book (400 words)
   - Step-by-step process
   - Link to booking page
   - WhatsApp booking option
   - 24/7 availability

8. FAQ (400 words)
   - 8-10 common questions
   - Answers with links

9. Conclusion & CTA (200 words)
   - Summary of benefits
   - Call to action
   - Booking links
```

#### Step 3: Write Draft (2-3 hours)
**Option A: Hire Writer**
- Send outline + brief
- Writer delivers draft
- You edit

**Option B: Write Yourself**
- Follow outline
- Write naturally (expand each section)
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use bullets and lists for scanability
- Include examples and data
- Natural keyword placement

**Option C: Use AI + Edit**
- Use ChatGPT with prompt:
  ```
  Write a 4,000 word comprehensive guide on "Electric Cabs in Pune"
  for the website greenevcabs.com. Follow this outline: [paste outline]

  Include:
  - Keyword "electric cab pune" naturally 8-10 times
  - Real data/statistics
  - Natural internal links
  - Persuasive tone
  - Clear sections with H2 headings
  - FAQ section at end
  ```
- Copy output
- Edit for tone/accuracy
- Check for repetition
- Verify all facts

#### Step 4: Optimize for SEO (1 hour)
- [ ] **Meta Title** (60 chars max)
  Example: "Electric Cabs Pune Guide | Best EV Taxi Service | Book Online 24/7"

- [ ] **Meta Description** (155 chars max)
  Example: "Complete guide to electric cabs in Pune. Learn why choose EV taxi, pricing, booking process, and coverage areas. Save ‚Çπ500+ vs regular cabs!"

- [ ] **H1 Tag** (should have primary keyword)
  "Electric Cab Service in Pune - Complete Guide to EV Taxis"

- [ ] **Image Optimization**
  - Add featured image (1200x630px)
  - Use descriptive filename: electric-cabs-pune-guide.jpg
  - Add alt text: "Electric cabs parked in Pune ready for service"
  - Add 2-3 supporting images
  - Optimize all alt texts

- [ ] **Internal Links** (8-10 minimum)
  Link to:
  - Hinjewadi electric cab page
  - Wakad electric cab page
  - Kia Carens page
  - BYD eMax page
  - Comparison pages
  - FAQ pages
  - Pricing page
  - Booking page

  Use natural anchor text: "electric cab service in Hinjewadi" not "click here"

- [ ] **External Links** (2-3)
  Link to authoritative sources:
  - Government EV info
  - Industry reports
  - Relevant news

#### Step 5: Add Schema Markup (30 mins)
Add to your page's HTML:
```json
<script type="application/ld+json">
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Guide",
  "headline": "Electric Cabs Pune Complete Guide",
  "description": "Complete guide to electric cabs in Pune",
  "author": {
    "@type": "Organization",
    "name": "GreenEVCabs"
  },
  "image": "https://greenevcabs.com/image.jpg"
}
</script>
```

#### Step 6: Publish & Promote (1 hour)
- [ ] Publish to website
- [ ] Check it looks good on mobile
- [ ] Test all links work
- [ ] Update sitemap.xml
- [ ] Submit to Google Search Console:
  - Request indexing
  - Check if indexed within 24-48 hours

- [ ] Promote:
  - Share on WhatsApp status
  - Post on Facebook business page
  - Post on Instagram
  - Share in LinkedIn
  - Email to customer list
  - Add to homepage featured section

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### 1. ‚ùå Keyword Stuffing
**Wrong:**
"Electric cabs pune electric taxi pune ev cabs pune best electric cabs pune..."

**Right:**
"GreenEVCabs offers electric taxi service throughout Pune. Our eco-friendly cabs provide affordable transportation solutions..."

### 2. ‚ùå Thin Content
**Wrong:** 500-word articles on topics that deserve 2,000+ words

**Right:** Comprehensive, detailed guides (2,000-4,000 words minimum for important topics)

### 3. ‚ùå Poor Internal Linking
**Wrong:** Random links to random pages

**Right:** Strategic linking - pillar to cluster pages, cluster pages back to pillar

### 4. ‚ùå Forgetting Mobile
**Wrong:** Design for desktop, mobile breaks

**Right:** Test every page on mobile (your Astro site is good for this)

### 5. ‚ùå No Optimization After Publishing
**Wrong:** Publish and forget

**Right:** Monitor rankings, optimize pages ranking #4-10 to move to top 3

### 6. ‚ùå Inconsistent Publishing
**Wrong:** Publish 10 articles then stop for 2 months

**Right:** Consistent schedule (2-3 per week minimum)

### 7. ‚ùå Ignoring Reviews
**Wrong:** Don't ask for reviews or respond

**Right:** Ask every customer, respond to all, address concerns

### 8. ‚ùå Copying Competitors
**Wrong:** Copy competitor content or structure

**Right:** Create original, unique, comprehensive content better than competitors
